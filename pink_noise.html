Pink Noise

In Pink Noise, lower frequencies are much more audible than higher ones.

With these deeper tones emphazised, we can imagine that this noise (if visible) would have a warmer colour to it, giving it the name Pink Noise!

Pink Noise on a PSD Chart

Looking at the PSD chart, we can see that pink noise decreases in energy as the frequency gets higher.

This shows that the noise becomes less audible at higher frequencies because it has less energy!

What does it Sound Like?

Since Pink Noise has stronger presence in lower frequencies, it can sound similar to a calm rainstorm, or even wind swishing through trees!
