White Noise

White Noise mixes all audible frequencies evenly, from 20Hz to 20kHz

Similar to white light, it encompasses all visible colors. Its name derives from this connection!

White Noise on the PSD Chart

If we look at the PSD chart, we can see that the noise stays consistent, no matter the range of frequency.

In simpler terms, every frequency in white noise has an equal level of intensity!

What does it sound like?

White noise can be compared to listening to TV static or a vaccuum buzzing.

Improve Sleep Quality

A recent systematic review combinining 36 studies of white noise concluded that 50% of those listening to the noise while sleeping had a more restful sleep, and woke up less during the night. This improved their day-to-day schedules largely. You can read more about this review here!

Block Out Environmental Noises

Since white noise consists of every audible frequency the human can hear, it acts as a natural block, tuning out other environmental noises. This may be beneficial when trying to focus! You can learn more about the noise's studying benefits here!
